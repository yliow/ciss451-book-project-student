  \phi(mn) = \phi(m)\phi(n) \cdot \frac{g}{\phi(g)}

  where $g = \gcd(m,n)$.
  (Note that the above does \textit{not} assume $m,n$ are coprime.
  What is the above if $m,n$ are coprime?)
