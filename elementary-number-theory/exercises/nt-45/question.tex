  Leetcode 372.\\
  \url{https://leetcode.com/problems/super-pow/} \\
  Your task is to calculate $a^b \pmod{1337}$ where $a$ is a positive integer
  and $b$ is an extremely large positive integer given in the form of an array.
  For instance
  for $a = 2, b = [1,0]$,
  the output is $1024$.
