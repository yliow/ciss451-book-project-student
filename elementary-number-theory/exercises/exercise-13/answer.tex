\SOLUTION

1. Let $x,y,z$ be integer solutions.
Taking mod 4, we get
\begin{align*}
      x^2 \equiv 3z^2 \pmod{4}
\end{align*}
Therefore
\[
  x^2 \equiv 0, \,\,\,\,\, z^2 \equiv 0 \pmod{4}
\]
This implies that $4\mid x^2$ and $4 \mid z^2$, which implies that
$2\mid x$ and $2 \mid z$, i.e., $x,z$ are even.


2. Let $x = 2a$, $y = b$, $z = 2b$. We get
\begin{align*}
  5(2a)^2 - 8b^2 = 3(2c)^2 
\end{align*}
which gives us
\[
  5a^2 - 2b^2 = 3c^2  
\]
