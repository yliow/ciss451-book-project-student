(a)
We have
\begin{align*}
a * x = a * y
&\implies a^{-1} * (a * x) = a^{-1} * (a * y) \\
&\implies (a^{-1} * a) * x = (a^{-1} * a) * y & & \text{by associativity axiom} \\
&\implies e * x = e * y                     & & \text{by inverse axiom} \\
&\implies x = y                             & & \text{by neutrality/identity axiom}
\end{align*}

Proof for (b) is similar to (a).
\qed
