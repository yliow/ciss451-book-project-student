  \textnormal{(\defone{Cancellation laws})}
  Let $(G, *, e)$ be a group and let $a, x, y \in G$.
  The following holds:
  \begin{myenum}
    \item $a * x = a * y \implies x = y$ 
    \item $x * a = y * a \implies x = y$
  \end{myenum}
