  Let  $(G, *, e)$ is a group and let $H$ be a subset of $G$. 
  Then $H$ is a subgroup of $G$ iff
  \begin{myenum}
  \item $H$ is nonempty
  \item $x, y \in H \implies x * y \in H$
  \item $x \in H \implies x^{-1} \in H$
  \end{myenum}
